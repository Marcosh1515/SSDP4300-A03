How to Use the To-Do List

Add a To-Do

  1. Go to the "To-Do List" page
  2. Type your task in the input field
  3. Click the "Add Task" button
  4. Your task will be saved to DynamoDB and displayed in the list
  5. The application also support

Edit a To-Do

  1. Click on the To-Do's corresponding "Edit" button
  2. Change the text in the input field
  3. Click on "Update Task"

Delete a To-Do

  1. Click on the To-Do's corresponding "Delete" button
